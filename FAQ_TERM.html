Terminology

FPS
FPS stands for 'First Person Shooter'. It is a genre of video games which involves shooting from the perspective of the first person.
Platforms
Platforms are devices that allowed users to play video games. For example, a platform could be a computer, or a console.
AI
AI stands for 'Artificial Intelligence'. AI is used in games that involve non player characters.
